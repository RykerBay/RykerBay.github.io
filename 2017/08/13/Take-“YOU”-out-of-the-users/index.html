Take “YOU” out of the users

Take “YOU” out of the user （换位思考）

  • Find your user and their goals
  • The user is ??? , who wants to ???.
  • Like ‘Em
  • “Thinking of your “mom”（who just like your user)” in order to make your product easier to understand.
  • Talk to real people
  • Talking to lots of people, potential customers. Not mean asking them “how about your product”, instead asking what they would do to achieve your product’s goal.(像 预约餐馆的app，要先找餐馆。看使用者原来是怎么找的）
  • Put in down on paper
    用下面的方法做笔记
  • personas (角色模型） ———————— Who
  • scenarios (情景） ———————— What they want
    *user flows ———————— How to get it (ps.类似work flows)

Wireframes and prototypes (改善设计)

流程

设计模型

  • low-fidelity 线框 + 少描述
  • high-fidelity 线框 + 详细描述
  • annotated 线框 + 详细描述（点击事件，操作结果等)
  • prototype 可操作（仿真实的产品）

Tips for working faster

  • 使用素材库（网络上的），积累组建自己的素材库
  • Make important content real(不需要把所有的地方都仿真）
  • Don’t only make homepage （各个level都要做）
  • Learn to make it wrong，尽快碰到错误的地方，尽快改（设计本来就是一个流程不断循环的过程)
